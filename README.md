# Transparent-Home-Services-BA-Case-Study
This case study focuses on transforming the fragmented Indian home services market into a structured, execution-driven model. The project addresses critical pain points such as pricing opacity, lack of accountability, and project delays through the design of a centralized digital platform.
Key Business Objectives
•	Transparency: Introduce standardized City-wise Rate Cards and BOQ transparency.
•	Quality Control: Implement digital validation via photo evidence and milestone-based escrow payments.
•	Efficiency: Reduce service cancellations from 25% to <10% through automated assignment.
